How to make Banana Pudding!!

Description

Banana putting is the perfect desert for any occasion! The smooth taste of banana served as a cold dish is everyone's perfect desert! Enjoy our recipe and I hope you love it!!

Ingredients

  • 2/3 cup white sugar
  • 1/3 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 tetaspoon salt
  • 3 large eggs, beaten
  • 2 ups milk
  • 2 tablespoons butter, softened
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 bananas, peeled and sliced
  • 1/2 (12 ounce) package vanilla wafer cookies

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Combine sugar, flour, and salt together in a medium saucepan. Add eggs and stir well. Stir in milk, and cook over low heat, stirring constantly.
  3. When the mixture has thickened enought to coat the back of a metal spoon, remove from heat and continue to stir, cooling slightly. Stir in butter and vanilla until smooth.
  4. Layer pudding with bananas and vanilla wafers in a serving dish.
  5. Chill at least 1 hour in the refrigerator before serving.
  6. Enjoy!!!
