How to make Ham and Biscuits!!

Description

Ham and biscuits are a delicious alternative for breakfast!! Enjoy with any type of beverage in the morning and make your belly happy!!

Ingredients

  • Can of Pillsbury Dough biscuit
  • 3 slices of country ham
  • butter
  • Skillet

Steps

  1. Open up the Pillsbury dough biscuit can.
  2. Lightly butter up the top part of the uncooked biscuits
  3. Put biscuits inside the oven and let them heat up for 10 minutes on 430 degree temperature.
  4. Take out biscuits and add another coat of butter on top.
  5. Slice the biscuits in half horizontally.
  6. Add the slices of country ham and then add the top.
  7. Enjoy!!
