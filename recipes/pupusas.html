How to make Pupusas!!

Description

A pupusa is a Central America dish that is easy to make. Please use the following ingredients and steps to make a delicious pupusa!

Ingredients

  • Masa harina
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 cup queso fresco

Steps

  1. Stir the masa harina and water together until smooth. Let dough rest 5 to 10 minutes.
  2. Shape dough into 2 inch diameter balls. On lightly floured surface, roll out ball into 6 inch diameter. Sprinkle 1/4 cup queso fresco over each round. Place second tortilla over cheeseh and pinch edges togetherto seal cheese.
  3. Heat ungreased skillet over medium-high heat. Place one tortilla into skillet at a time, and cook untill cheese melts and tortillas are lightly browned, about 2 minutes on each side.
  4. Enjoy!!
